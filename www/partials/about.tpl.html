Welcome

MOOCs4U - Free online courses, aka Massive Open Online Courses (MOOCs) from different providers in one place.

In this app you will be able to:

  1. Find MOOCs from the best universities in the world, such as Harvard, MIT, Stanford and others, offered via platforms such as Coursera, Udacity, edX and others. More platforms will be added soon.
  2. Access to course details such as course summary, start date, prerequisites and introductory videos to help you decide whether to sign up.
  3. Sign up for the MOOC of your choice by just tapping the Sign Up button.
  4. Organize MOOCs into those that you have just signed up for and those that you have completed.
  5. Add your favorite MOOCs to a WatchList.
  6. Rate MOOCs to help others make their choices.
  7. Share MOOCs via Facebook, twitter and email.
  8. Chat with other MOOC students in the spirit of peer-to-peer collaborative learning
  9. Keep up-to-date with the fast moving MOOCs world by reading the most important MOOCs news.
